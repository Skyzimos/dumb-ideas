New Sight Reading Packet Causes Mass Panic, Emotional Collapse

What started as a regular Tuesday ended in mass panic when Coon Rapids High School Choir Director "Mr. Kainz" handed out the "Bruce Phelps Level IV" sight reading packet.

Eyewitnesses report that the moment the packet was distributed, the room instantly dropped 12 degrees, with one student reportedly asking, "If I pretend to faint, do I still get a participation grade?"

At this time, we were only able to collect two witness statements, both of which were members of the Tenor section.

Tenor section member Logan described the experience;

“I sight read one measure and had to check if I still had a pulse. I’m pretty sure reading it out loud summoned something. Maybe Beethoven? All I can say is I haven’t been the same since.”

The bass section tried to escape through a side door but tragically, it was locked. When they returned to their seats, Mr. Kainz simply smiled and said, “Page 4,” without question.

Tenor section member Desmond stated in an interview that

“I’ve been through Bruce Phelps Level III. But Level IV? That’s the beginning of the end. I feel like I owe Bruce Phelps money and this packet is how he’s collecting.”

The school’s counselor’s office confirmed a 700% spike in emergency walk-ins, most clutching the packet and whispering, “I swear the notes are moving.”

Choir Director Mr. Kainz was unavailable for comment, but was last seen calmly sipping tea out of a mug labeled:

“It Builds Character.”

Authorities warn that Bruce Phelps Level V exists — and if the rumors are true, it includes three key changes, singing backwards, and rhythms that don’t even line up, just for funsies.

We're all doomed.
